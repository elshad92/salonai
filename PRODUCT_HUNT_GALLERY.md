# Product Hunt Gallery — Screenshot Guide

5 screenshots for the PH gallery. Standard size: **1270 × 760 px**.

Tools: **CleanShot X** (Mac) or **Snipping Tool** (Win + Shift + S → set to 1270×760) or browser DevTools → device toolbar set to 1270×760.

---

## Screenshot 1 — Dashboard (Hero)

**File:** `ph-01-dashboard.png`

**What to capture:**
- Open the Owner Dashboard at `/dashboard`
- Make sure the stats cards show numbers (Today / Week / Month bookings)
- The AI Analyst card should be visible with a plain-English insight
- Recent appointments list visible below

**Why it matters:** First impression. Shows the product is real and data-driven, not a mockup.

**Tip:** If the stats are zeroes, add 1-2 test appointments from the Booking page first.

---

## Screenshot 2 — AI Receptionist Chat Widget

**File:** `ph-02-chat-widget.png`

**What to capture:**
- Open any page (e.g., `/dashboard` or the Salon public page `/s/<slug>`)
- Click the gold chat bubble (bottom-right) to open the widget
- Have a short 3-4 message conversation ending with the confirmation message
- The widget should show a confirmed booking message with ✅

**Why it matters:** This is the flagship "wow" feature — shows AI booking in action.

**Tip:** Type "I want a haircut tomorrow at 3pm, I'm Alex, +1 555 1234" — the AI will confirm quickly.

## Screenshot 3 — WhatsApp / Telegram AI Administrator

**File:** `ph-03-ai-admin.png`

**What to capture:**
- Open the Messaging page (`/messaging`)
- Show the Telegram bot form with bot username filled in and the green "✓ Saved & webhook registered" status
- Alternatively: show a real Telegram conversation (phone screenshot) where the bot replies to a booking request

**Why it matters:** Proves 24/7 automation via the channel clients already use.

**Tip:** A real phone screenshot of the bot responding is more compelling than the settings form.

## Screenshot 4 — AI Marketer

**File:** `ph-04-ai-marketer.png`

**What to capture:**
- Open the Marketing page (`/marketing`)
- Click "Generate Content" and wait for 5 captions to appear
- Capture the full list of generated captions with "Copy" buttons visible

**Why it matters:** Demonstrates the breadth of the platform — it's not just booking, it's a full AI team.

**Tip:** Make sure your salon name is set in SalonSetup first so the captions mention it by name.

## Screenshot 5 — Landing Page / Pricing Section

**File:** `ph-05-landing-pricing.png`

**What to capture:**
- Open the Landing page (`/`)
- Scroll to the Pricing section showing the 4 plan cards (Solo $19, Small $49, Pro $99, Enterprise $199)
- Or capture the comparison section that shows savings vs. commission platforms

**Why it matters:** Leads with the core value prop — zero commissions, transparent flat fee.

**Tip:** Capture in a clean browser window (hide bookmarks bar, extensions). Full-width at 1270px.
